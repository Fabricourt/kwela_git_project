Lamu Port Development

In 1975 the Kenya goverment decided to construct a new port in lamu to complement the one in mombasa.

Lamu was choosen as a result of the sutability of lamu which has a deep natural harbour of 18m. This would see all the ocean faring vessels which cannot dock in Mombasa because of its depth docking in Lamu.

It was not until 2012 that the goverment of Kenya took the 1st step toward its realisation. IT saw the president of South Sudan and the prime minister of Ethiopia putting a commumeration stone at the place where the port would be constructed.
